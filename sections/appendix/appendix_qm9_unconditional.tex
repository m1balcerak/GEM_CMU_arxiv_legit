\section{Unconditional generation on QM9}
\label{app:qm9-uncond}

Table~\ref{tab:uncond-qm9} reports unconditional metrics on QM9. V.U. is saturated around 100\%, and
lower FCD is often paid for with decreased novelty or larger networks that can memorize the training
set.
